About Us

Welcome to Eraya Travels. Expertly Crafted Journeys, Delivered Seamlessly At Eraya Travels, we design seamless, personalized travel experiences that reflect your interests and priorities. Headquartered in Mumbai and established in 2025, we offer comprehensive travel services—covering everything from international holidays and domestic getaways to customized itineraries and corporate travel bookings. Our founders bring over 10 years of professional experience in travel management, enabling us to deliver expert guidance and meticulous attention to every detail of your journey. Whether you’re exploring the world or managing business travel, we make the process effortless and efficient.

  • Tailored Itineraries
  • End-to-End Travel Management
  • Domestic & International Travel Solutions
Our Mission

To redefine travel through precision planning, personalized service, and reliable execution—helping clients explore the world with peace of mind.

Our Philosophy
